Gravity

A 3rd person platformer where the player manipulates gravity to traverse the levels.

This game was developed as a schoolproject in the Unity Engine. The player can switch the direction of gravity and learns that most plabes can turned in to a walkable surface

The player then have to utilize the power of gravity to traverse through the levels and overcome obstacles and puzzles along the way

I was part of the design team developing the different levels and the puzzles in them. I was also responsible for all music and sound in the project
