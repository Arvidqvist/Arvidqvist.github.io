Professor Dino

Educational point and click game for kids.

Developed as a school project using the Unity engine. The goal was to use game based learning and integrate it in a seemless and fun way.
The game was about teaching kids about the different dinosaurs that lived during the cretacious period.

The first element we used were two "Drag and drop" sections where the player had to drag the correct attribute to the dinosaur on the picture and later on placing the dinosaur in the correct continent.
To finish the game off we test the students knowledge with a quiz based around the knowledge they have just learned.

i was part of the programming team and took care of all programming revolving aroung the drag and drop sections of the game.
